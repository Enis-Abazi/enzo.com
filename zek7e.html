Zek7e Quizz

Zek7e is a quizz for all ages.

Zek7e was created in 2021 in purpose of challenging people with their knowlege . The question there are scientific , basic life question , and school question. If you finish the quizz at the end you'll get the certeficate for completing it without mistakes.

This Quizz was offered by a lot of public schools and private schools to publish it and challenge their students , so we made it publish so everyone can play our quizz for free by just visiting our webpage.

Zek7e blue background color represents easy question , because blue is more likely calm color that helps people relax their minds and focus on the question , while yellow represents a medium question that may take a minute to focus on the question , somehow its good color to be focused on.

And the red question represents the hard question , which may take a while for you to answer it right , if you fail you'll get back to the homepage of the Zek7e Quizz , which you need to start from the beggining.
